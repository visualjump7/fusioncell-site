Expertise Across Every Domain

Your Fusion Cell team brings specialized knowledge to every category of your holdings.

Real Estate

Every Property, Fully Managed

Residential homes, commercial holdings, vacation properties, and development projects. Your team maintains current valuations, tracks operating budgets, coordinates maintenance, and manages insurance across every property in your name.

Property valuations and market tracking Maintenance scheduling and vendor coordination Operating budget management Insurance and tax obligation tracking
Aviation

Full Cost-of-Ownership Visibility

Aircraft ownership involves operating budgets, maintenance schedules, crew management, fuel costs, hangar fees, and insurance. Your team tracks every element and gives you a clear picture of what your aircraft costs to own and operate.

Operating budget and cost-per-hour tracking Maintenance schedule and compliance oversight Crew coordination and management Hangar, fuel, and insurance management
Marine

Vessels Managed Season to Season

From dock fees and crew management to maintenance cycles and seasonal operations, your team handles every aspect of vessel ownership. Insurance, registration, and compliance are tracked and kept current without your involvement.

Dock fees and marina coordination Crew management and scheduling Maintenance cycles and refit planning Insurance and registration compliance
Vehicles

Collections and Daily Drivers

Whether it's a collection of rare automobiles or your family's daily vehicles, your team manages registration, insurance, maintenance schedules, storage, and current valuations across every vehicle.

Registration and insurance tracking Maintenance scheduling and service records Storage coordination and climate control Valuation tracking and provenance records
Collections

Cataloged, Valued, and Protected

Art, wine, watches, memorabilia — your team catalogs every piece, maintains current valuations, coordinates insurance coverage, manages storage conditions, and tracks provenance documentation.

Comprehensive cataloging and documentation Valuation tracking and appraisal coordination Insurance coverage and claims management Storage, climate control, and provenance
Insurance & Legal

Cross-Holdings Coordination

Insurance policies, legal documents, compliance requirements, and renewal schedules span across every category of your holdings. Your team coordinates it all, ensuring nothing expires, lapses, or goes unaddressed.

Policy management and renewal tracking Legal document organization Compliance monitoring across domains Cross-holdings insurance optimization
