Your Window Into Your Team's Work

The Fusion Cell app puts everything your team is managing in one secure, private place.

Dashboard

Your Team's Latest Work, Front and Center

The dashboard surfaces what matters most. Total holdings value, upcoming obligations, pending decisions, and cash flow summaries — all maintained and updated by your Fusion Cell team.

Holdings

Every Property, Aircraft, Vessel, Vehicle, and Collection

Browse every holding your team manages. Current values, detailed records, budget access, and filters by category or location. Your team keeps everything current so you always see the latest picture.

Budgets

Detailed Operating Costs for Every Holding

Your team uploads detailed operating budgets for every holding. See annual costs, fixed and variable breakdowns, category-level detail, and monthly tracking. Know exactly what everything costs to own and operate.

Cash Flow

Every Invoice and Obligation, Tracked

Your team tracks every invoice, payment, and financial obligation across your holdings. Calendar views, status tracking, and payment history give you full visibility. Nothing comes as a surprise.

Decisions

Context, Recommendation, One Tap

When something needs your attention, your team puts it in front of you with full context and a recommendation. Approve or reject with one tap. Every decision is recorded with a complete audit trail.

AI Assistant

Ask Anything About Your Holdings

A built-in assistant with full awareness of everything your team has loaded into the app. Query your holdings, budgets, cash flow, and decisions using natural language. Get answers grounded in your actual data.

Your Data Is Private

Your Fusion Cell app is configured exclusively for you. No shared infrastructure, no cross-principal data, no compromises on security. Your information stays yours.
